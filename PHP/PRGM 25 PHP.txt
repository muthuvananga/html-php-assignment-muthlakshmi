<?php
$text = "The Thing will come to you soon";

// Convert first 'the' (case-insensitive) to 'best'
$updatedText = preg_replace('/\bthe\b/i', 'best', $text, 1);

echo "Original: $text<br>";
echo "Updated: $updatedText";
?>


OUTPUT:

Original: The Thing will come to you soon  
Updated: best Thing will come to you soon
